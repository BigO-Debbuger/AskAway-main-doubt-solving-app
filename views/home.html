RoadMap Feature

The Roadmap Section on our website offers curated learning tracks for various tech stacks, including Front-end, Back-end, Full Stack, Data Science, and more. Each track features a structured curriculum, recommended resources, and practical projects tailored for different skill levels. Additionally, learners can access video tutorials and participate in live sessions for real-time interaction with instructors. This section fosters a community for discussion and collaboration, empowering users to navigate their tech journey effectively.

Ask a Doubt Feature

The Ask a Doubt Section on our website allows users to post their queries and receive expert guidance from mentors. Users can submit their questions, provide context, and attach relevant files to receive detailed explanations and solutions. This feature enables learners to clarify their doubts, enhance their understanding, and progress in their learning journey. Additionally, users can engage in discussions, share insights, and collaborate with peers to foster a supportive learning environment.

Book a Session Feature

The Book a Session Section on our website enables users to schedule one-on-one sessions with mentors for personalized guidance and support. Users can select mentors based on their expertise, availability, and user ratings to book sessions that align with their learning goals. This feature facilitates direct interaction, feedback, and mentorship, allowing users to seek guidance, clarify concepts, and enhance their skills effectively. Additionally, users can provide feedback, rate mentors, and share their learning experiences to improve the platform's mentorship services.

Perk System

The Perk System rewards mentors based on user reviews. After each session, students can rate mentors, and positive reviews translate into perks or rewards. This system motivates mentors to provide high-quality support, improving overall engagement and learning on the platform. When a mentor reaches a certain number of perk units, their Mentor Level increases, boosting their profile and reputation on the platform as a trusted expert.

Income Source

Skilled freshers or the teacher who want to educate students online too can earn on the platform by becoming mentors. As they assist students and receive positive reviews, they gain Perk Units, which strengthen their profile and increase earning opportunities. More the mentor level, more the cost per session.

AI Support

This project by Full Stack Force integrates AI for personalized, real-time doubt resolution through AI Mentorship. AI helps automate routine responses with chatbot integration, enhancing the user experience by providing fast query handling and personalized learning paths. This integration ensures efficient, timely support, improving overall learning outcomes.
